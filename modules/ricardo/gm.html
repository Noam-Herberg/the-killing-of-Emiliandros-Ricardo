DM Notes

The Ricardo Affair

Retired Astra Militarum Captain Emiliandros Ricardo was murdered in his hab unit. Moments later the hab was destroyed in a controlled explosion designed to conceal evidence.

Multiple factions are now searching for Ricardo's hidden stashes, each containing dangerous secrets capable of exposing crimes, heresy, corruption, or worse.

The players begin at Ricardo's mortuary viewing just as armed attackers arrive.

Open NPC Stats

What Actually Happened

Timeline

Years Ago

  • Ricardo retires from the Astra Militarum after severe injuries.
  • Purchases a bar in the hive.
  • Begins working with a street gang called the Vipers distributing obscura.
  • Uses Vipers to abduct Molly's sister after Molly threatens to expose him.
  • Molly remains trapped managing the bar while her sister is held under Viper protection.

Later

  • Ricardo is approached by corrupt securitors known as The Hand.
  • The Hand use Ricardo's bar and properties as safe hubs for xenotech smuggling.
  • Xenotech supplied primarily by a cold trader named Hamo.
  • Ricardo becomes wealthy and increasingly connected.

Xenotech Clients

  • Mariana 0490 purchases xenotech for radical research.
  • Magos Halicor becomes Ricardo's biggest customer.
  • Ricardo becomes romantically involved with Mariana.

The Tome

  • Ehira Quihaj secretly arranges Ricardo's sale of the Mortis Abomina to the cult Observatory of the Open Eye.
  • Ricardo discovers the tome is genuinely dangerous.
  • He refuses to complete the transaction.
  • Ricardo hides the tome in one of his secret stashes, intending either to destroy it or surrender it anonymously to authorities.

The Murder

  • The Vipers want Ricardo punished for ending obscura operations.
  • They manipulate Molly using her captive sister.
  • Molly murders Ricardo and his confidant Renzo with a stub pistol.
  • She flees the scene.

The Explosion

  • Magos Halicor learns Ricardo possessed a Tesseract Vault.
  • Halicor deploys a detonation servitor through the hab ventilation systems.
  • The explosion destroys most evidence.

Immediate Aftermath

  • Local enforcers begin investigation.
  • The Hand seize control and reassign investigators.
  • The Hand realise the explosion was too precise for gang retaliation.
  • High-ranking nobles and enforcers tied to the cult force The Hand out.
  • The cult faction recovers damaged dataslate fragments.
  • The cult attempts to reconstruct and decrypt the dataslate to locate the tome.

Major Factions

Competing Agendas

1. The Vipers

Street gang specialising in obscura distribution.

Goals

  • Recover Ricardo's obscura routes.
  • Retain control of Molly.
  • Continue using Ricardo's bar for narcotics distribution.
  • Silence anyone linking them to the murder.

Key Facts

  • Kidnapped Molly's sister years ago.
  • Manipulated Molly into killing Ricardo.
  • Double-crossed Molly afterwards and kept her sister anyway.

Potential Encounters

  • Drug dens.
  • Underhive ambushes.
  • Interrogations.
  • Protection rackets.

2. The Hand

Corrupt securitors and enforcers.

Goals

  • Protect xenotech smuggling operation.
  • Find replacement smuggling hub.
  • Recover incriminating ledgers.
  • Avoid exposure.

Key Facts

  • Worked with Ricardo.
  • Linked to cold trader Hamo.
  • Initially believed Vipers caused explosion.
  • Growing worried about deeper conspiracy.

Potential Encounters

  • Corrupt enforcer raids.
  • Surveillance.
  • Intimidation attempts.
  • Internal divisions among members.

3. Observatory of the Open Eye

Occult noble cult seeking daemon summoning.

Goals

  • Recover the Mortis Abomina.
  • Complete ritual involving The Door Beneath Starlight.
  • Identify who manipulated them.
  • Summon daemon entity.

Key Facts

  • Possess damaged dataslate fragments.
  • Currently reconstructing encryption keys.
  • Include high-ranking nobles and officials.

Potential Encounters

  • Masquerade gatherings.
  • Ritual sites.
  • Warp phenomena.
  • Assassins and occult agents.

4. Magos Halicor

Radical tech-priest descending into paranoia.

Goals

  • Unlock secrets of the Tesseract Vault.
  • Eliminate Mariana.
  • Erase all evidence of xenotech network.
  • Silence surviving witnesses.

Key Facts

  • Ordered the hab explosion.
  • Increasingly unstable.
  • Obsessed with Vault contents.
  • Will eventually begin murdering associates.

Potential Encounters

  • Servitor attacks.
  • Mechanicus facilities.
  • Forbidden laboratories.
  • Warp-tainted technology.

Secret Stashes

Ricardo's Hidden Evidence

1. Military Trophy Stash

Contains Purinan 442nd memorabilia and a pict recording of Hogan Reams disposing of Commissar Henrietta's corpse.

Importance: ruins Hogan if discovered, and provides a valuable emotional connection to Ricardo's past.

2. The Mortis Abomina

Contains a heretical tome, warp lore, and references to The Door Beneath Starlight.

Importance: main cult objective and risk of daemon summoning.

3. Obscura & Money Stash

Contains drug reserves, Thrones/Gelt, and Viper financial records.

Importance: motivates Scratch and provides key leverage against the Vipers.

4. Xenotech Stash

Contains xenotech artefacts, a ledger of xenotech buyers and transactions, and evidence implicating The Hand and Halicor.

Importance: the most politically explosive stash and key evidence against the smugglers.

Stash Location Clues

How They Can Find Them

Cult Dataslate

Contains an encrypted clue to one stash location.

Renzo's Tattoo

Contains a coded clue to one stash location, hidden inside a tattoo on Renzo's body.

Twist: Renzo was Ricardo's partner in crime and main bodyguard alongside Hammer. His body is currently in the custody of The Hand, who removed it from the scene before the cultists arrived.

Molly

Knows the location of one stash. Refuses to cooperate unless her sister is rescued.

Ricardo's Study

Contains a coded clue to the final stash location.

Potential puzzle: military cipher, Guard field code, or hidden map in old campaign records.

Major NPCs

Pressure Points

Molly

Bar manager. Publicly bitter, frightened, and evasive.

Secret: murdered Ricardo and Renzo, motivated by coercion and desperation.

Possible outcomes: redeemed, arrested, killed, or helps players against Vipers.

Renzo

Ricardo's partner in crime, closest confidant, and main bodyguard alongside Hammer.

Status: murdered alongside Ricardo.

Use: coded tattoo clue, personal effects, witness statements, flashbacks, or recordings.

Hamo

Cold trader and original xenotech supplier.

Role: possible late-game antagonist or escape target.

Player-Specific Connections

Personal Stakes

  • Hogan Reams wants the pict recording destroyed.
  • Hammer wants revenge and knows the cult visited Ricardo.
  • Mariana wants xenotech evidence erased and is at risk from Halicor.
  • Scratch wants the obscura stash and regrets the final argument.
  • Ehira accidentally caused the entire affair and wants the tome recovered secretly.
  • Cali Hargrove is investigating the cover-up and is the closest thing to a lawful investigator.

Possible Endings

  • Rescue Molly's sister.
  • Defeat Vipers.

  • Expose or destroy The Hand.
  • Kill or stop Halicor.

  • Vault opened.
