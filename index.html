Inquisitorial Incident File

The Killing of Emiliandros Ricardo

Three nights ago, an explosion tore through a mid-hive hab block belonging to retired Astra Militarum Captain Emiliandros Ricardo.

Open Character Dossiers

Mortuary Intake Summary

Officially, the blast was caused by a faulty gas line.

The blast killed Ricardo instantly and caused extensive damage to the surrounding district. Official reports attributed the incident to a faulty gas line, though locals have already begun circulating their own theories. In the crowded depths of the hive, violent deaths rarely go unquestioned for long.

To most citizens, Ricardo was simply another ageing Imperial veteran. Once an officer of the Purinan 442nd Infantry, he had settled on the planet after injuries forced his discharge from the Astra Militarum. Those who knew him described him as charismatic, stubborn, and unusually well-connected for a retired guardsman.

Now he is dead.

As required by custom, Ricardo's body has been transferred to the mortuary chambers beneath Saint Drusus Memorial Hospital ahead of cremation rites. Though the gathering is unofficial, word has spread among those who knew him that visitors are being permitted to pay respects before the body is processed.
